This is your digital identity, you should fill your name (full name, organization name, etc.), email and your avatar (logo, profile picture).

webc-app-identity is an internal component used by webc-app-menu.

From webcardinal.json:

"identity": { "avatar": "/assets/logo.svg", "name": "MyOrganisation", "email": "MyOrganisation@example.com" }

Default values are set in default.ts;
