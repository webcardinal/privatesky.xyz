This component should be used when a modal is needed in your application.

From a custom Controller of your page, who inherits the WebController or Controller from WebCardinal.controllers:

this.showModal("Content", "Modal title", onConfirm, onClose); this.showErrorModal("Message", "Error title", onConfirm, onClose); this.showErrorModalAndRedirect("Message", "Redirecting title", "contact-page-tag", 3000); this.showModalFromTemplate("modal-template", onConfirm, onClose);
